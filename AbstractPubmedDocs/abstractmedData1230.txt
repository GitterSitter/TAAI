Br J Hosp Med (Lond). 2008 Jan;69(1):13-7.Surgery for acute heart failure syndromes.Large S(1).Author information: (1)Papworth Hospital, Papworth Everard, Cambridge CB3 8RE.This article describes the role of surgery in the management of acutepresentations of heart failure. Intervention is set in the context of medicalmanagement. If the cause of the failure is not apparent this help is limited tomechanical support as intra-aortic balloon pumping, heart assistance orreplacement. The most dependable management for refractory, severe heart failure remains heart transplantation but is dependent upon donor organ availability.
