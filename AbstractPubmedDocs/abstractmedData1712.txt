Rev Esp Anestesiol Reanim. 2001 Dec;48(10):473-5.[Anesthesia for heart transplantation].[Article in Spanish]Van Besouw J(1).Author information: (1)Consultant in Cardiothoracic Anaesthesia, St. George's Hospital, Londres,Reino Unido. jpvb@gas.mailbox.co.ukThis review covers the management of patients proposed for heart transplantation from the moment of preoperative assessment. Drug treatments that provide a bridgeto transplantation are emphasized, with mention of traditional drugs such asdobutamine, more recent agents such as phosphodiesterase inhibitors, and finally new drugs such as levosimendan. Mechanical support devices and indications fortheir use leading to transplantation are discussed. Finally, the notion ofxenotransplantation is mentioned as a possible solution to the imbalance between supply and demand for transplantable organs.
