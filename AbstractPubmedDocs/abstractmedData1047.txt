Expert Rev Cardiovasc Ther. 2009 Sep;7(9):1067-77. doi: 10.1586/erc.09.86.The future is here: ventricular assist devices for the failing heart.Alba AC(1), Delgado DH.Author information: (1)Heart Failure and Transplantation, University Health Network, NCSB 11C-1201,585 University Avenue, Toronto, ON, M5G 2N2, Canada. carolina.alba@uhn.on.caMechanical circulatory support is an important adjunct to the management ofpatients with advanced heart failure. Technological advances in this area haveimproved overall survival and decreased the incidence of complications. Inaddition, they have expanded the population suitable for this therapy. Thechallenge for clinicians is to translate the clinical evidence into the selectionof the most appropriate device that will benefit an individual patient. Thispaper will review ventricular assist devices currently available and theirclinical indications.
