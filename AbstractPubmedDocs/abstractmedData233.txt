Asian Cardiovasc Thorac Ann. 2014 Feb;22(2):205-7. doi: 10.1177/0218492312469200.Epub 2013 Jul 11.Use of Rotaflow pump for left ventricular assist device bridging for 15 weeks.Khaliel F(1), Al Habeeb W, Saad E, Kjellman U.Author information: (1)Section of Cardiac Surgery, Heart Centre, King Faisal Specialist Hospital and Research Centre, Riyadh, Saudi Arabia.Mechanical circulatory support systems have been described for short- andlong-term left ventricular assistance. We report the use of a Rotaflowcentrifugal pump in a 55-year-old man with ischemic cardiomyopathy and severeleft ventricular dysfunction. He was successfully bridged to transplantation with15 weeks of Rotaflow support, with no major adverse events.
