Cardiac transplantation remains the final extraordinarytherapeutic option for the treatment of truly irreversible end-stage heartfailure in all age groups. The selection process of candidates and the acceptanceof patients with relative contra-indications is characterized by the experienceand skills of an interdisciplinary transplant team, which should have access todifferent mechanical circulatory support systems for short-term or long-term use:bridging to transplant as well as for recovery.
