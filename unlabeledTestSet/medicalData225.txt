We present the case of a patient who developed cardiogenic shock after PCI and      was supported with intra-aortic balloon counterpulsation and the Levitronix      CentriMag short-term mechanical circulatory support. He was evaluated for      implantation of a ventricular assist device as bridge-to-transplantation.      Preoperative transesophageal echocardiography revealed a massive thrombus of the       aortic root and ascending aorta despite optimal anticoagulation regimen.
