Implantation      provides immediate hemodynamic restoration and clinical stabilization, leading to      end-organ recovery and thus eventually allowing cardiac transplantation.      Occasionally, implantation of a total artificial heart is not feasible for      anatomical reasons. For this patient group, we have developed an alternative      technique using the paracorporeal Thoratec biventricular support system      (Thoratec, Pleasanton, CA, USA) as a modified total artificial heart.
