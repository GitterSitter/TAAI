If it does not improve with appropriate medical therapy, we must consider      mechanical support. Recently, extracorporeal membrane oxygenation (ECMO) has      shown better results than a right ventricular assist device or retransplantation.      Two HT patients with hypertrophic cardiomyopathy had cold ischemic times beyond      >240 minutes. After HT, their right heart function worsened and was unresponsive       to medical therapy. After our application of ECMO, weaning was successful and the      patients were discharged without complication. Early application of ECMO for RHF       after HT is a good option.CI  - Copyright (c) 2013 Elsevier Inc.
