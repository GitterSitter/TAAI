A 38-year-old      man with non-ischemic dilated cardiomyopathy was admitted in stage D heart      failure. Preoperative computed tomography demonstrated multiple saccular      aneurysms of the ascending aorta and sinus of valsalva. We successfully performed      complex aortic surgery and the implantation of a continuous-flow LVAD (HeartWare       HVAD) (HeartWare International, Framingham, MA). The postoperative course was      uneventful.
