The TandemHeart initially had been used for      temporary hemodynamic assistance during postcardiotomy heart failure and      high-risk coronary interventions. More recently, its use in patients with      cardiogenic shock from acute myocardial infarction, fulminant myocarditis, and      critical aortic stenosis has been reported.
