He was initially supported by extracorporeal membrane oxygenation      and subsequently was converted to paracorporeal support with a Levitronix left      ventricular assist device. He experienced multiple postoperative complications      including renal failure, respiratory failure, retroperitoneal hematoma requiring       suspension of anticoagulation, and fungal bloodstream infection precluding      transition to an implantable device. He was reconditioned and successfully      underwent orthotopic heart transplant 183 days after presentation.
