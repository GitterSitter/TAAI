LVAD support can cause reverse left ventricular (LV)      remodeling that results in the improvement of LV function and allows LVAD      removal. We present a case of successful removal of a DuraHeart LVAD because of      sufficient recovery of LV function. Before LVAD removal, we conducted an \"LVAD      weaning test\" by decreasing pump speed and performing an additional normal saline      infusion test.
