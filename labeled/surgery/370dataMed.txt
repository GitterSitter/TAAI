Experimental studies have shown that RV function is      derived from 2 sources: the free wall of the RV and the interventricular septum.       Management of RV failure involves not only decreasing RV afterload, but also      optimizing both contributions to RV function, which is best achieved by      optimizing developed systemic pressure.
